Establishing the PCC Molecular and Genomic
Research Laboratory, Biobank, and Tumor Bank

1-2 April 2025 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM Institute of Biology Auditorium, UP Diliman

About

The Philippine Cancer Center (PCC), in collaboration with the Biobank Core Facility of the Philippine Genome Center (PGC), University of the Philippines, is undertaking an initiative to assess and develop the landscape for molecular and genomic research, biobanking, and tumor banking in the Philippines.

Date: April 1-2, 2025

Location: Institute of Biology - Auditorium, UP Diliman

Upcoming Events

Training and Knowledge Sharing

An informative session where participants enhanced their understanding of biobanking practices and standards.

Laboratory Sample Preparation

Researchers carefully preparing samples in the lab to ensure high-quality results for genomic research.

Workshop Highlights

A memorable moment during the Biobank Workshop, fostering collaboration and shared learning experiences.

Facility Inspection in Progress

A collaborative team inspecting the upcoming facility, ensuring it meets the highest standards for research and biobanking.

Successful Planning Session

Our team and key stakeholders gathered to discuss operational frameworks and sustainability plans for the facility.

Site Walkthrough with Experts

A detailed walkthrough of the facility with experts to align infrastructure with research needs.

Biobank Core Facility Dinner Gathering

The Biobank Core Facility invites all stakeholders to a formal dinner at UP Hotel on April 1, 2025, from 6:00 to 8:00 PM. This gathering marks the beginning of our two-day workshop, providing an opportunity to connect, collaborate, and strengthen our shared commitment. Join us for an evening of insightful discussions and professional networking.

Accommodation Information: To ensure a comfortable and seamless stay, we have curated a list of recommended hotels near the venue. These accommodations have been selected for their convenience, accessibility, and quality service.
